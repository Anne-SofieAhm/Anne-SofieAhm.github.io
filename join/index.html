Graduate studies at SEOS

Are you interested in Earth history research? Currently, we are looking for motivated students to join the Earth history group! Please reach out to Dr. Ahm to discuss opportunities for Masters and PhD projects (starting in 2022).
There are no deadlines in applying for admission to the School of Earth and Ocean Sciences. There are three entry points: January, May and September. The Faculty of Graduate Studies does indicate that applications should be submitted as early as possible and that no assurance can be given that domestic applications received after May 31 or international applications received after December 15 can be processed in time to permit registration in the following Winter Session (Fall term).

Postdoctoral Research

While we do not have allocated funding to hire postdoctoral researchers, there are opportunities to apply for indenpendent funding through different agencies. If you are interested in pursuing postdoctoral research please reach out, and we can discuss opportunities, research projects, and funding sources.

  • Read more about the Banting Postdoctoral Fellowships here. Canadian citizens, permanent residents of Canada, and foreign citizens are eligble to apply and the application deadline is normally at the end of April.

  • Read about NSERC Postdoctoral Fellowships program here Please note this fellowhip is only eligble for Canadian citizens and permant residents. Application deadline normally in mid October.
