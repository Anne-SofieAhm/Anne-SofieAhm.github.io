Graduate studies at SEOS

Are you interested in Earth history research? Currently, we are looking for motivated students to join the Earth history group! Please reach out to Dr. Ahm to discuss opportunities for Masters and PhD projects. While there are no fixed deadlines in applying for admission to the School of Earth and Ocean Sciences, it is good to reach out to potential faculty advisors early. There are three entry points for admission: January, May and September. The Faculty of Graduate Studies does indicate that applications should be submitted as early as possible and that no assurance can be given that domestic applications received after May 31 or international applications received after December 15 can be processed in time to permit registration in the following Winter Session (Fall term).

Postdoctoral Research

We do not have allocated funding to hire postdoctoral researchers, but there are opportunities to apply for indenpendent fellowships through different federal agencies. If you are interested in pursuing postdoctoral research please reach out, and we can discuss opportunities, research projects, and funding sources.

  • Read more about the Banting Postdoctoral Fellowships here. Canadian citizens, permanent residents of Canada, and foreign citizens are eligble to apply and the application deadline is normally at the end of April.

  • Read about NSERC Postdoctoral Fellowships program here Please note this fellowhip is only eligble for Canadian citizens and permant residents. The application deadline is normally in mid October.
